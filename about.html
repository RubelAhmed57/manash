Creative, Passionate Programmer and All Around Nerd

Hi, I think you already know my name. So let's talk about something else, I was born on February 9th at Dhaka, Bangladesh.

In short I enjoy writing, eating, coding and watching movies/tv-series in my spare time. I have been writing articles online since 2010. I mostly write in Bengali, but now I've been writing on my medium blog in English too.

Besides, I love to read books, online technical and non technical articles. My hobby is to play Tabla and design vector art.
